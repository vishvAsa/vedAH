<details><summary>Whitney</summary>

### Translation
11. "O refreshment, come! O *svadhā́*, come! O pleasantness, come! O  
thou rich in cheer (*írā*), come!"

### Notes
Ppp. combines *svadhe ’hi* and *sūnṛte ’hi*, and omits *iti* at the end  
(as in §9).
</details>
