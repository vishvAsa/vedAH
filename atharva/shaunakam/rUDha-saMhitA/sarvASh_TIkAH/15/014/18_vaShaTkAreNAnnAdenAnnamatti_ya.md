⌊18.⌋ with the cry *váṣaṭ* as food-eater doth he eat food who knoweth  
thus.
</details>

<details><summary>Griffith</summary>

He who hath this knowledge eats food with the exclamation Vashat as food-eater.
</details>

</details>

</details>
