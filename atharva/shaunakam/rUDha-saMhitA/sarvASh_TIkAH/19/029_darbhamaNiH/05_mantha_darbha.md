<details><summary>Whitney</summary>

### Translation
5. Grind, O *darbhá*, my rivals; grind those etc. etc.

### Notes
About half the mss. accent *manthá*. The comm. gives, as if from the  
*dhātupāṭha, mantha loḍane* (Westergaard and Böhtlingk *viloḍane*).
</details>
