<details><summary>Whitney</summary>

### Translation
27. To the *viṣāsahí*, hail!

### Notes
The seventeenth book begins with the word *viṣāsahím;* and this time the  
comm. takes the trouble to specify that "the seventeenth *kāṇḍa*" is  
intended. ⌊Cf. p. 805, ¶1.⌋
</details>
