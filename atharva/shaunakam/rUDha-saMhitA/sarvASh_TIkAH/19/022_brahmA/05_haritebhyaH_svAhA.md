<details><summary>Whitney</summary>

### Translation
5. To the green ones, hail!

### Notes
Two of our mss. (O.D.) accent with our text *háritebhyas;* SPP. reads  
*haritébhyas*, with (apparently) all his authorities and nearly all of  
ours.
