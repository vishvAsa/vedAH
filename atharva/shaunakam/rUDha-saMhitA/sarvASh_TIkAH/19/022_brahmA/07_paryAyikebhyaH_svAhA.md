<details><summary>Whitney</summary>

### Translation
7. To them of the *paryāyas*, hail!

### Notes
</details>

<details><summary>Griffith</summary>

To those composed in strophes, Hail!
</details>

<details><summary>पदपाठः</summary>

प॒र्या॒यि॒केभ्यः॑। स्वाहा॑। २२.७। 
</details>

</details>

</details>
