<details><summary>Whitney</summary>

### Translation
7. Hew down (*vraśc*), O *darbhá*, my rivals; hew down those etc. etc.

### Notes
</details>

<details><summary>Griffith</summary>

Tear thou my rivals, Darbha, etc.
</details>
