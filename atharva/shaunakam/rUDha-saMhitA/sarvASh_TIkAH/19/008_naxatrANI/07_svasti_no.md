<details><summary>Whitney</summary>

### Translation
7. Let well-being be ours; let fearlessness be ours; homage be to  
day-and-night.

### Notes
The verse is wanting in Ppp.

</details>

<details><summary>पण्डित क्षेमकरणदास त्रिवेदी - भावार्थः</summary>
