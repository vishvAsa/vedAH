<details><summary>Whitney</summary>

### Translation
10. Free from defilement (*-riprá*) \[are\] the waters; let them  
\[carry\] away from us defilement;—

### Notes
</details>

<details><summary>Griffith</summary>

May stainless waters cleanse us from defilement.
</details>
