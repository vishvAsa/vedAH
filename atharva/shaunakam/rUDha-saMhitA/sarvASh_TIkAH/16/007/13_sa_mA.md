<details><summary>Whitney</summary>

### Translation
13. Let him not live; him let breath quit.

### Notes
This verse also forms a part of x. 5. 25 etc.
</details>

<details><summary>Griffith</summary>

Let him not live. Let the breath of life forsake him.
</details>
