Regarding अपिगणम्-

List of all अ"पि-compounds attested with accent- (source: Grosses Petersburger Wörterbuch von Böhtlingk und Roth)

1. अपिकक्ष" - the area of the armpits and shoulder blades
2. अपिकर्ण" - the area of the ear
3. अ"पिभाग - having a stake
4. अ"पिव्रत - involved in the व्रत
5. अपिशर्वर" - either end of the night
6. अपिपक्ष" - the area of the page

Where अ"पि is an उपसर्ग on account being associated with a क्रिया-
7. अपिज" - born later
8. अपिधा"न - covering
9. अ"पिनद्ध - fastened
10. अपिप्रा"णी - filling

The applicable सूत्र for our case is तत्पुरुषे तुल्यार्थतृतीयासप्तम्युपमानाव्ययद्वितीयाकृत्याः, which places accent on the पूर्वपद (अ"पि)। 7 - 10 have other rules. I am yet unable to find the सूत्र for explaining the accent in 1, 2, 5, 6. But clearly the structure of our targeted meaning is present in 3 or 4, where अ"पि is accented, and not in 1, 2, 5 or 6. So अ"पिगणम् is the form with correct accent.
