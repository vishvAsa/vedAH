## Honey

Source: [TW](https://x.com/blog_supplement/status/2031975732327354636)

Honey was the only sweetener known through much of the vedic age. It also functioned as a key metaphor in the language. It's mention however, varies widely even among related texts ,which paradoxically highlights its importance as a metaphor.
