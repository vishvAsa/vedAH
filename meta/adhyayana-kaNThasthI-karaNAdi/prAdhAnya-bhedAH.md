## प्राधान्य-केन्द्र-विवेकः
Excessive focus on memorization turns adhyeta-s into glorified autistic tape-recorders. 

Similarly focus on vikRti-pATha-s (beyond krama) is unproductive. There are more important uses for one's intellect.

## सम्प्रदाय-भेदः
mAdhva-s do widespread vedAdhyayana as well - minus memorization. That approach seems more practicable in the current era.
