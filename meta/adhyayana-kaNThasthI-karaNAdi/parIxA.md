## राजमहेन्द्रपुरम्
> The rajamundhry veda pariksha was started in the year 1937 by the Hota family (street is named after them in Rajamundhry called Hota Vari street). Shri Mahaperiyava had done chaaturmaasyam in that house where the exams were held for a long time. Now its held in datta kshetram. The "patta" carries immense value in Andhra and other places due to the severity of the exam. Only those who hold the "patta" are allowed to participate in the various veda sadas held across different places in Andhra. 
> 
> Regarded as one of the toughest veda exam in the country, the Rajamundhry veda exam is a nightmare to veda adhyayees in terms of the strictness of the exam. Out of 40 odd applicants only 6 or 7 passed the exam successfully. The exam was gruelling 3hr session for my son with not even a water break in between. A total of 180 questions. - Source: [TW](https://x.com/yajurveda_hari/status/1828341829462372718)
